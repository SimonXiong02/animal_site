Frequently Asked Questions

What colours do silkie chickens come with?

There are two variants of silkie chicken the bearded and non-bearded. The most common colours are black and white.

Are silkie chickens better than normal hens?

Yes, they have advantages over regular chickens.
The most iconic advantage is that they can be keep as pet due to their unexpected friendliness and IQ.

How long does silkies live for?

They usually have a life span of 7 to 9 years on average.
Some of them can live up to 10 to 13 years.

Where do silkies orignate from?

They were originally from China.

How does silkie chicken got its name?

Their name comes from one of Marco Polo's diary logs.
