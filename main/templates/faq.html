Frequently Asked Questions

What colors are ordinary silkie chickens?

There many variants of silkie chickens, the most common colors are black and white.

Are silkie chickens smarter than normal hens?

Yes, they have advantages over regular chickens.
The most iconic advantage is that they can be keep as pet due to their unexpected friendliness and IQ.

What is the average life expectany of Silkies?

Silkies usually live for 7 to 9 years, though some can reach 10 to 13 years.

Where did Silkies come from?

They were originally from China.

How did the Silkie chicken get its name?

Silkies were first mentioned in ancient Chinese writings as chickens with fur-like feathers.
Marco Polo saw them in Asia in the 1200s and wrote about their hair-like feathers,
which helped the West learn about this unique breed.
